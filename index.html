About Us

compU is a completely free service that helps people to learn the essentials to survive in the computer-driven society we live in.

Contact Us

Learn to succeed

Computers are necessary to be successful in today's society. We provide you with a place where you can find everything you need to know about computers.

The Goal

compU is founded on the idea that everyone in the world should be computer literate. That is why we provide the same service in different languages! In order to achieve our goal we allow experts around the world to contribute to this service by providing simple tutorials in one of our supported languages. If you think you can make a significant contirbution to this community click here!
